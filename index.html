Star Wars opening crawl recreated in HTML, CSS & JS by Charlie Gerard

A long time ago in a galaxy far,
far away...

It is a period of agile development. EX devs, coding from level 5, have won their first victory against caffeine deficiency.

During the sprint, BAs managed to create ingenious plans for the Alliance's ultimate weapon, AFTERNOON TEA.

Driven by grumbling tummies, our heroes race towards the kitchen, seeking sustenance that can restore balance to the Force....
