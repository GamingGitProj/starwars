EPISODE I: BEYOND THE RIM

Amid a backdrop of a galactic empire, a huge space station known as the WHEEL is one of the few places still beyond the Emperor's reach, attracting low-lives, fortune-seekers, and refugees alike.

Meanwhile, a derelict message pod discovered by an illicit tech company on the Wheel gives new life to old smugglers' stories about a long-lost Separatist treasury ship, the SA NALAOR. The wreck lies at the edge of wild space, waiting to be found by those brave or crazy enough to look....
